Frequently Asked Questions

When is the best time to visit Namibia?

The dry season (May to October) is best for wildlife viewing, while the wet season (November to April) offers lush landscapes and fewer tourists.

Do I need a visa to visit Namibia?

Most visitors can obtain a 90-day tourist visa on arrival. Check with your local Namibian embassy for specific requirements based on your nationality.

Is Namibia safe for tourists?

Namibia is generally very safe for tourists. However, take normal precautions as you would when traveling anywhere, especially in urban areas.

What vaccinations do I need?

Recommended vaccinations include Hepatitis A & B, Typhoid, and routine vaccines. A yellow fever certificate is required if arriving from affected countries.

Can I use credit cards in Namibia?

Credit cards are widely accepted in cities and major tourist areas, but it's advisable to carry cash for remote areas and smaller establishments.

What is the time zone in Namibia?

Namibia is in the West Africa Time Zone (WAT), which is UTC+1 during standard time and UTC+2 during daylight saving time.
